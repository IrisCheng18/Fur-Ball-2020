Saturday, October 3rd

@ 6:00 - 11:00 P.M.

Event

San Diego Humane Society's Fur Ball gala is San Diego's most memorable and beloved dog-friendly gala,. Now in its 34th year, this black-tie, dog-friendly event will celebrate our shared compassion for animals in need with an evening under the stars.

Well-dressed animals and their pet-loving parents enjoy a night of fun and festivities while supporting the lifesaving programs and services of San Diego Humane Society. The Fur Ball brings together over 600 passionate and engaged animal lovers from throughout San Diego County. Loyal guests return year after year to experience the excitement of bringing their cherished dogs along to support a lifesaving cause.

The Fur Ball kicks off with a cocktail reception and continues with live and silent auctions, live music and dancing. A touching program celebrating the animals and programs of San Diego Humane Society accompanies an exquisite vegetarian meal prepared by Renowned chef Luca Zannoni of Civico 1845. Dinner is complemented by fine wines and desserts. The evening concludes with an after-party featuring dancing, doughnuts and after-dinner espresso.

Fur Ball 2020 is going to be held on Saturday, October 5, 2019 from 6 to 11 pm.

Fur Ball 2020 is going to be held at San Diego Humane Society’s San Diego Campus @ 5500 Gaines St., San Diego, CA 92110.
