The Fur Ball is an innovative and engaging way to reach a dedicated base of animal lovers, all while demonstrating your commitment to pets and wildlife in need. With your support, San Diego Humane Society will change the lives of nearly 50,000 animals this year by providing shelter and lifesaving veterinary care, rehabilitating injured wildlife, investigating animal cruelty and neglect, offering supplies and resources to keep pets with their families and so much more.
